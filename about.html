MKG North Dublin is a fun and friendly Martial Arts club based in Skerries, North County Dublin. From small beginnings we have been growing in popularity. Classes for Kids, teens and adults there is a class for everyone who would like to learn and grow.
Coaches James & Courtney bring a wealth of experience and a passion for teaching martial arts.
Part of the international MKG Martial Arts organisation which was set up by Guro Rick Faye in 1982. Our club is the first Irish club to join the MKG family.

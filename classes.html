Titans (4-7yr) . . . a fun filled class introduces the basics of kickboxing, games and movement.

Warriors (8-11yr) . . . develop further kickboxing skills and introduce Kali drills with sticks.

WarriorsX (12-15yr) . . . opportunity to develop advanced martial arts. Kickboxing, Jeet kune do, Kali

Adult Kicboxing (16yr+) . . . Kickboxing fundamentals blending elements of Fipino martial arts, Kali and Muay Thai boxing.

Adult JKD & Kali (16yr+) . . . Jeet Kune Do - Bruce lee's fighting system combines elements of western boxing and kung fu.
Kali - Filipino Martial Art uses both weapons and empty hand.
